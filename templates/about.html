About Our Project

Understanding how we predict cold risks using science and technology

Our Mission

The Common Cold Predictor was developed to help people understand their susceptibility to colds based on genetic and environmental factors.

How It Works

Our prediction model analyzes multiple factors including:

  • Genetic markers (blood type and genotype)
  • Local weather conditions
  • Air quality data
  • Demographic information
